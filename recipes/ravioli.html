Ravioli

Recipe Description

This is a delicious italian classic. Enough to fill up the whole mob

This is what you need!

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Minced Beef
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Lay down your pasta sheets in your greased baking dish
  2. layer cheese, sauce, beef
  3. repeat!
  4. top off with cheese and sauce mixed
  5. bake!
